Expand description

The InputWindow keeps memory of the input hits and fourier related data

Fields§

§window: VecDeque<u128>

The window keeps track of times of hits

example:

  • first hit at time 100

  • second hit at time 120

  • third hit at time 150 then we have the resulting window: (50, 20, 0)

  • fourth hit at time 170 then we have the resulting window: (70, 50, 20)
